Adventure World Orlando delivers thrilling experiences for the entire family with world-class roller coasters, immersive themed lands, and spectacular live shows. The park spans 200 acres featuring eight distinct zones, each with unique attractions, dining, and entertainment options.

Adrenaline seekers can't miss the Dragon's Fury coaster, reaching speeds of 75 mph with seven inversions. Families enjoy the Safari Trek boat ride through animatronic wildlife and the Fantasy Kingdom area with gentle rides for younger children. The evening parade and fireworks show are absolute must-sees.

Multi-day tickets offer the best value, allowing guests to experience everything without rushing. Fast Pass options minimize wait times for popular attractions. The park has numerous dining venues ranging from quick-service counters to full-service restaurants with character dining experiences. Summer months see extended hours and special events.
