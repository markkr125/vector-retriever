The Atlantis Royal Dubai represents the pinnacle of ultra-luxury hospitality in the Middle East. This architectural marvel features underwater suites with floor-to-ceiling aquarium views, where guests sleep surrounded by 65,000 marine animals including sharks, rays, and exotic fish species.

The resort boasts 17 world-class restaurants including three Michelin-starred establishments, with celebrity chefs Nobu Matsuhisa and Gordon Ramsay heading signature venues. The 90,000 square meter waterpark Aquaventure offers thrilling slides, a private beach, and swim-up bars serving premium cocktails.

The award-winning ShuiQi Spa spans three floors with 27 treatment rooms, Turkish hammams, crystal steam rooms, and an ice fountain. Private cabanas overlook the Arabian Gulf where guests enjoy butler service, champagne, and sunset yoga sessions.

Families appreciate the kids' club with supervised activities, teen gaming lounge, and ambassador lagoon where children can interact with dolphins. Business travelers utilize the 1,800 square meter ballroom and state-of-the-art conference facilities. Helicopter transfers from Dubai International Airport take just 15 minutes.
